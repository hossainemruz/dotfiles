Run `taskctl context` and read `artifacts.task`. Treat `$ARGUMENTS` as requested refinements or context to evaluate against the Task. Check goal, acceptance criteria, constraints, non-goals, conflicts, edge cases, and validation expectations without changing scope. If the Task is clear and no requested refinement needs applying, make no edits. Otherwise, ask questions one at a time until all ambiguity and unknowns are resolved. Propose your recommendation along with other options. Once everything is clear, apply valid requested refinements and update `task.md`; preserve its structure and prose.

Return Task ID/title/path, verdict, blockers, and `/research` or `/plan`.
